The Tulagi wordmark

Included · in scope

The Tulagi wordmark is made up of uniquely hand drawn lettering that feels human and reflects the distinct spirit of Boulder. The wordmark works as a single color, bold and graphic design. Each application is shown in black and reversed white.

Primary application — wordmark
Primary, blackWordmark
Reversed, white on darkWordmark
Serif hallmarks

Based on our conversation, the wordmark incorporates subtle hallmarks of serif typography the team liked. There is a deliberate contrast between the thicker and thinner strokes of the letterforms, echoing the way a serif typeface is constructed.

One cohesive unit

The letters fit together into a single cohesive unit rather than sitting as separate characters. The shapes are customized to flow into one another, most visibly where the T meets the U and where the L meets the A.

Secondary application — circle seal

The second application in your scope is a circular seal: the icon set into a stamp shape with the festival name, location, and year. This alternate iteration may be applied for badges, coins, and small merch.
